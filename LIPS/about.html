About Us

LIPS (Languages Interpreting Peoples Services)

LIPS (Languages Interpreting Peoples Services, LLC) was established in 2018, Michigan, USA with the mission of breaking barriers and building bridges among people with limited proficiency language speaking of different cultures. Our determination is to support you communicate across barriers in language and culture among multilingual population.

Communication is the key component to build human relationship and translating and interpreting different languages is an easy means to achieve the desired goal. Translating and interpreting brings interdisciplinary people with different languages under one umbrella. The core purpose is to provide integrated interpreting and translation services, commitment, add value and aspire team of experts to deliver products efficiently and timely.

This concept of understanding a language and its interpretation initiated to establish the organization. Under this umbrella diverse needs of the people from all walks of life connect to communicate, understand, and bridge each other. Our focus is to ensure delivery of services to people to their satisfaction.
